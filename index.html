About Me

I'm Noy Shaer, an architect with a passion for creating innovative and sustainable designs that meet the needs and desires of clients, while also contributing positively to the built environment. I received my M.Arch at NYIT, specializing in Urban Design, and I feel passionate about creating better cities for people.

My approach to architecture integrates functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability, resulting in spaces that are not only beautiful but also practical and environmentally responsible. Through thoughtful consideration of site context, user experience, and environmental impact, I create designs that stand the test of time.

With 5 years of professional experience, I've had the opportunity to work on various project types, from residential homes to commercial spaces and public buildings. Each project presents unique challenges and opportunities, which I embrace with creativity and technical expertise.
