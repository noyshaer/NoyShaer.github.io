About Me

I'm Noy Shaer, an architect with a passion for creating innovative and sustainable designs. I received my M.Arch at NYIT, specializing in Urban Design, with a focus on creating better cities for people.

My approach to architecture integrates functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability. With 5 years of professional experience, I've worked on residential, commercial, and public building projects.
