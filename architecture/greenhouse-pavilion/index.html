Greenhouse Pavilion

location: Technion, Israel

Design: 2023

Social Greenhouse Pavilion Competition – awarded second place

The pavilion was designed around the concept of reusing construction materials found across the Technion campus, which is currently undergoing extensive renovation and expansion. Following a site survey identifying available materials and their quantities, we designed a single-story lightweight structure built from reused wooden beams, pallets, blocks, reinforcement meshes, and metal rods. Inspired by the form of agricultural greenhouses, the design allows natural light and ventilation to minimize energy use. The western facade faces Ullmann Building’s pathway and serves as an angled exhibition wall, with perforated mashrabiya surfaces inviting curiosity. The interior can be adapted for different uses, with potential future upgrades such as solar panels and recycled concrete bricks.
