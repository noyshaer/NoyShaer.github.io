Kishon River Flow

location: Kishon River, Haifa, Israel

Design: 2024

Academic Project

The project is a rowing club located on the Kishon River in Haifa. During the site analysis, I examined the form of rowing movement, a horizontal motion that inspired the building’s massing. The structure consists of floating volumes layered above one another, creating a sense of horizontal flow. Movement within the building is guided by yellow elements that lead toward the project’s main spaces. These elements are expressed through the shading system, structural framework, and ventilation design. One of the key design principles was maintaining accessibility to the river and ensuring a continuous walking path along it. Additionally, the building includes public gathering areas overlooking the river while preserving the privacy of its users.
