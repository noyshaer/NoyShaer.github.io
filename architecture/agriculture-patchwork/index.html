The Fabric of Fields

location: Northern Negev, Israel

Design: 2024

Dori Competition – Finalist

The Agricultural Mosaic is located in a region rich in landscapes, heritage, and agriculture that serves as both an economic and cultural foundation. Situated near Gilat Junction, the project creates a dynamic meeting point between people, landscape, and diverse programs, functioning in both routine and emergency situations. It reflects Israel’s social and agricultural diversity through a flexible complex that can transform from a rest hub for soldiers during emergencies to a community market (“Northern Negev Market”) in everyday life. By bringing together soldiers, farmers, and residents, the project becomes an economic, social, and cultural anchor within the open landscape of the Negev and the country as a whole.
